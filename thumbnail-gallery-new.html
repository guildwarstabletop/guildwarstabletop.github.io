The Ranger


Rangers are long-ranged combatants that use
archery, beast mastery, and their attunement
to nature to fight from a distance and survive.

  • Expertise (Primary)
    •  The energy cost of skills is decreased.

  • Beast Mastery
    •  Travel and fight with an animal
    companion.

  • Marksmanship
    •  Execute attack maneuvers with bows
    or other ranged weapons.

  • Wilderness Survival
    •  Use natural rituals, preparations,
    and traps to outwit your foes.
